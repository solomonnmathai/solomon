About
me

Hello! I’m Solomon Mathai, a passionate graphic designer based in India.
With a keen eye for detail and a love for creativity, I specialize in crafting
visually compelling designs that tell a story.

My journey in graphic design began with a fascination for colors, shapes,
and the endless possibilities they offer in visual communication. Over the
years, I’ve honed my skills in various design disciplines, including branding,
print design, and digital media. My experience spans creating brochures,
logos, websites, and more, each project reflecting my commitment to quality
and innovation. I believe that great design is not just about aesthetics but
also about solving problems and enhancing user experiences.
